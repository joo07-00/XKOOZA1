XKOOZA Brand Story

The Birth of Urban Fashion

We are a group of 6 friends united by diverse educational backgrounds and a shared passion for fashion. Three of us started in the American Diploma program, where the initial spark for XKOOZA was born. What began as one friend's creative vision quickly ignited the enthusiasm of others, leading to a collaborative journey in fashion design.

After careful consideration and numerous experiments, we settled on the name "XKOOZA" - a name that perfectly embodies our vision and unique approach to style. The addition of three more friends from high school completed our dynamic team, bringing fresh perspectives and diverse talents to the table.

Our Fashion Journey

Our breakthrough moment came when The Olympus approached us to create jackets for their graduation ceremony. We seized this opportunity as the perfect launchpad for our brand, marking the official beginning of XKOOZA.

Today, we stand committed to our founding principle: creating clothing that transcends mere fashion. Each piece we produce carries its own unique story and idea, reflecting our dedication to making a meaningful impact in the fashion world.
